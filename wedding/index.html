Port Townsend is just over the water on the Olympic Penninsula and accessible by both car and ferry.

Edmonds to Kingston Ferry

The Edmonds → Kingston ferry is an easy way to get to Port Townsend from Seattle and the surrounding area. The ferry ride is very pretty and costs around $30 for the round trip depending on how many people you have in the car. If you are not familliar with the ferry system, we are happy to provide tips!

Click "More options" on map above to customize and view detailed directions.

The Downtown Seattle → Bainbridge Island ferry is another alternative if you wish to come by ferry. It would likely take around the same amount of time.

Driving Around the Puget Sound

Port Townsend can also be reached by driving around the Puget Sound. This involves roughly twice as much driving as the ferry options if coming from Seattle, but depending on traffic and ferry wait times, usually only amounts to slightly longer travel times.

This is might be a preferable option if you are already coming from the south such as Oregon or SeaTac/Tacoma.
