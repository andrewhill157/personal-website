Port Townsend is just over the water on the Olympic Penninsula and accessible by both car and ferry.

Driving Around the Puget Sound

Port Townsend can also be reached by driving around the Puget Sound. This involves roughly twice as much driving as the ferry option if coming from Seattle, but depending on traffic and ferry wait time takes only slightly longer in total travel time.

This is might be a preferable option if you are already coming from the south such as Oregon or SeaTac/Tacoma.
